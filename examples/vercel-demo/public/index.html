How it works

One turn, three honest steps

No black box. Every answer travels the same short pipeline — and stops at your content's edge.

Step 01

Retrieve

Your pages become a searchable index. Each question pulls the passages that actually answer it.

Step 02

Ground

One model pass writes the reply from those passages only — no invented facts, every claim citable.

Step 03

Route

When intent turns serious, it captures the lead and hands the conversation to a human — you.

Why Kenalin

Trusted by design, and yours to shape

Easy to adopt

One <script>, a create-kenalin scaffold, or the @kenalin/* packages. Live in minutes, no lock-in.

Fully customizable

Brand colors per light/dark, swap every icon, set the persona, toggle modules — adapt it to your site without forking.

Evidence-grounded

Every answer cites a source from your own content. If it isn't there, Kai won't claim it.

Private & self-hosted

MIT-licensed, runs on your own infrastructure. No database for a fresh install; secrets stay in your env. Speaks EN & ID.

Roadmap

Grounded today, deeper tomorrow

The build order follows the value: better retrieval and memory first, richer knowledge and learning next.

Now Shipped & live
  • Real retrieval over your own content
  • Response & retrieval caching (Redis)
  • Token & context budgets per turn
  • Bilingual EN / ID · one-script + npm embed
Next In progress
  • Explicit context caching
  • Structured, graph-aware knowledge
  • Real token streaming
Later Exploring
  • A learning loop that improves from real chats
  • No-code config studio
  • More model providers (Anthropic, OpenAI)
