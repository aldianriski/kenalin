Meet Kenalin AI Assistant

Kenalin is an open-source, embeddable AI assistant that introduces you and your work — grounded in your own content, and routing serious leads to a human. The chat in the corner is Kenalin, explaining Kenalin.

View on GitHub

Why Kenalin

Evidence-first

Every claim is grounded in your public content and cited with a source card. No made-up facts.

Guided, not open-ended

It reads intent each turn and routes toward a useful next step — including a human handoff.

One script tag

Configured in one file, embedded with one <script>. Themeable to your brand.

Open & self-hosted

MIT-licensed, runs on your infrastructure, no database required for a fresh install.
