# What Kenalin can do

Kenalin is built from **seven modules** you toggle independently in config:

- **Portfolio Discovery** — answer questions about projects and background, with source cards.
- **Hiring Assistant** — help a recruiter judge role fit from real work history.
- **Lead Qualification** — light, adaptive screening (a few questions, never a price).
- **Service Matching** — map a visitor's need to what the owner offers.
- **Contact Handoff** — route a meaningful conversation to a human with a short brief.
- **Calendar Booking** — surface a scheduling link when it's the right moment.
- **Page Context** — understand which page the visitor is on and tailor answers.

Every answer is **evidence-first**: if there's no supporting content, Kenalin says
so rather than making something up. It infers the visitor's **intent** each turn
(exploring, hiring, a business opportunity) and guides toward a useful next step.
It's **bilingual** (English + Indonesian out of the box) and themeable to your brand.
