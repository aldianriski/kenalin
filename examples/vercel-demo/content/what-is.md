# What is Kenalin?

Kenalin is an open-source, embeddable **AI introduction layer** for portfolio and
professional websites. It turns a static page into a guided, evidence-based
conversation: visitors can ask who you are, what you've built, and whether your
experience fits their need — and get answers grounded in your own public content.

Instead of a generic chatbot, Kenalin *introduces a specific person or team*. It
speaks about the owner in the third person, never pretends to be them, and always
backs claims with cited evidence. When a conversation gets serious — a hiring
lead, a project inquiry — it routes the visitor to a human through a channel you
configure (WhatsApp, email, calendar, or a webhook).

You add it with **one script tag** and configure everything in **one file**. No
backend team, no database required for a fresh install.
