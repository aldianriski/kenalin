# About Alex Rivera

Alex Rivera is a **product engineer and consultant** (a fictional persona for this
demo). Alex helps small teams turn rough ideas into shipped software — from the
first prototype to a maintainable product — with a focus on clarity, speed, and
tools people actually enjoy using.

Alex works across the stack: TypeScript and React on the front end, Node and
Postgres on the back end, and pragmatic infrastructure on top. Recent work spans
team productivity tools, e-commerce, and self-serve analytics.

Alex is available for **fractional engineering, product consulting, and short
build engagements**, and enjoys collaborating with founders and product teams.
